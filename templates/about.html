Salient Financial-Product Complaint Issues For Consumers Across Companies



This application aims to aid financial advisory firms, and their clients, by enabling consumers to explore notable complaint issues, and to compare and contast their prevalence across companies that provide a given financial product or service of interest. Consumers interested in a particular financial product can compare complaint issues across potential companies of interest, and thereby better fine-tune their dicussions and planning with financial advisors. The primary database utilzed for this app is the U.S. Federal government's Consumer Financial Protection Bureau Complaint Database.
